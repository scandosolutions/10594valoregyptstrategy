Usage Guidelines

Minimum Sizes

  • Digital: 60px wide (full lockup)
  • Print: 20mm wide (full lockup)
  • Icon only: 16px minimum

Clear Space

  • Minimum clear space equals the height of the "V" icon
  • Apply to all sides of the logo lockup
  • Keep area free from other elements
